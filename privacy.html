Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026  ·  Terms of Service →

The short version: Fliq processes your photos entirely on your device. We do not upload, sell, or share your photos or personal data with any third party. Your library stays yours.

1. Who We Are

Fliq is an iOS application developed and operated by Brian Lowen ("we," "us," or "our"). Our primary goal is to help you manage and clean up your iPhone photo library quickly and privately.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact form or open an issue on GitHub.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photo Library Access

Fliq requests access to your device's photo library through Apple's Photos framework (PhotoKit). This access is used solely to:

  • Display photos for your review within the app
  • Read metadata (creation date, file size, location if available) to sort and organize your review queue
  • Detect duplicate or similar images on-device using Apple Vision
  • Permanently delete photos from your library that you have explicitly approved for deletion

Your photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored outside of your device. All analysis is performed locally using Apple's on-device frameworks.

2.2 Purchase Information

If you purchase the Fliq upgrade, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit 2. We do not receive your payment card information, Apple ID, or billing address. We receive only a confirmation of the purchase from Apple, which we store locally on your device to maintain your unlock status.

2.3 App Preferences and State

Fliq stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and SwiftData):

  • Your review progress and session history
  • Your chosen review start date
  • Your preferences (batch size, notification settings, cadence)
  • Achievements and streak data
  • Your subscription tier status

This data is stored on-device only and may be backed up to iCloud if you have iCloud Backup enabled. We do not have access to this data.

2.4 Notifications

If you grant notification permissions, Fliq may send local push notifications to remind you to review photos according to your configured cadence. These notifications are scheduled on-device and do not require a server. No personal data is transmitted to generate notifications.

2.5 Diagnostic Alerts (Internal — Developer Use Only)

Fliq uses Microsoft Azure Communication Services (ACS) for an internal developer diagnostic pipeline. This system sends SMS alerts to the developer's private phone number when a user voluntarily submits a bug report via GitHub Issues. No personal user data, photos, or device identifiers are included in these alerts. The SMS number is registered solely for this internal developer tooling purpose, not for marketing to users.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, upload, or analyze your photos on any external server
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or Apple ID
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers
  • We do not sell your data to third parties — ever
  • We do not build advertising profiles or share data with data brokers
  • We do not collect crash logs through third-party services; any crash reporting uses Apple's standard mechanisms which you may opt out of through iOS settings

4. How We Use Your Information

The limited information stored locally on your device is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the app (photo review, smart grouping, recycle bin)
  • Remember your preferences and progress between sessions
  • Verify your purchase status to enable premium features
  • Send local reminders if you opt in to notifications

5. Third-Party Services

Fliq relies on the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: Processes all in-app purchases. Apple's Privacy Policy applies to all purchase transactions.
  • Apple PhotoKit / Vision: On-device frameworks for accessing your photo library and performing image analysis. No data leaves your device.
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): Used only if you opt in to notifications. No photos or personal data are transmitted.
  • Microsoft Azure Communication Services: Used exclusively for internal developer SMS diagnostics (see §2.5). Not used to contact users.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data associated with Fliq is stored on your device and is under your control. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset progress" option in Profile → Danger Zone to clear stats, sessions, and achievements
  • Uninstalling the Fliq app from your device, which removes all locally stored data

We do not hold any copy of your data on our servers, so there is no additional deletion process required on our end.

7. Children's Privacy

Fliq is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will address the situation promptly.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data through our servers, most data rights requests (access, correction, deletion) can be fulfilled directly on your device. However, if you have any privacy concerns, please contact us and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), California, or another jurisdiction with specific privacy regulations, your rights under applicable law (such as GDPR or CCPA) are respected. Given our data-minimal approach, exercising these rights typically means simply deleting the app.

9. Security

Because Fliq does not transmit your photos or personal data over the internet, the primary security surface is your device itself. We recommend keeping your iOS device up to date and using a device passcode or biometric authentication to protect your photo library.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are significant, we will notify users through an in-app notice. Your continued use of Fliq after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us
