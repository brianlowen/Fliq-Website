Features

Everything you need to take
back your storage

Fliq combines fast swipe-based review with smart detection to make photo cleanup feel effortless — even if you have 50,000 photos.

⚡️

Rapid-fire review

Swipe through photos at your own pace. Keep the memories, fliq the duplicates, blurry shots, and clutter. 10–50 photos per session, done in minutes.

🔍

Smart Groups

AI scans your library to surface duplicate bursts, screenshots, receipts, and more — grouped intelligently so you can clear an entire category in one tap.

🗑️

Recycle Bin

Nothing is deleted immediately. Flagged photos sit safely in your Recycle Bin, giving you time to reconsider before committing to a permanent delete.

📊

Storage dashboard

A real-time visual breakdown of how much space you're recovering. Track progress toward a cleaner library with a beautiful storage bar and library stats.

🏆

Stay motivated with milestones, daily review streaks, and unlockable achievements. The First Fliq, Storage Hero, and 1K Club await.

Your photos never leave your iPhone. All processing happens on-device using Apple's frameworks. Fliq only reads your library — it never uploads a single photo.

How it works

From cluttered to clean in minutes

Fliq makes photo cleanup feel less like a chore and more like a game. A few minutes a day adds up fast.

1

Set your start point

Tell Fliq where to begin — from the oldest photo in your library, or a specific date. Your progress is always saved so you pick up right where you left off.

2

Swipe through your library

Fliq shows you one photo at a time. Swipe right to keep, swipe left to flag for deletion. Session size is customizable — 10, 20, or 50 photos.

3

Run Smart Groups scans

Let Fliq find duplicates, near-duplicates, screenshots, and receipts. Review detected groups and decide what to keep — in bulk, in seconds.

4

Clear your Recycle Bin

When you're ready, permanently delete the flagged photos from the Recycle Bin and watch your available storage grow.
